About Army

Bts fandom is called Army or Bts-Army. It was officially established on July 9, 2013 after the first recruitment closed. Since then there have been many recruitment with many benefits ,like getting offical merch ,etc.

From 2013
"A.R.M.Y" stands for "Adorable Representative M.C. for Youth" and it carries quite some meaning behind it, given that "Army" is associated with the military, body armor, and how those two things are always together, the fandom name basically means that fans will always be together with BTS.
In 2017
the meaning was rebranded along with the logo, BTS and A.R.M.Y are almost mirrored mages of each other and they have a relationship that is affected by each other. The symbol of A.R.M.Y is expressed as a door on their viewpoint who is waiting & welcoming BTS on the opposite door when BTS opens a new door.

ARMY their fandom that has raised $200k for charity, organised more than 290 charity projects, saved more than 5000 lives, raised more than 1.5M for BTS uniceff campaign, planted around 50k trees and helped millions of people.
